What Are They?

Online shopping portals are platforms that allow you to earn bonus points, miles, or cash back when you make purchases at various online retailers. These portals partner with retailers and offer incentives to shoppers who access the retailer’s website through the portal.


How They Work: Here’s the basic process

  • Log in to your preferred online shopping portal (such as Rakuten, MyPoints, or others).
  • Browse the list of participating retailers.
  • Click on the retailer where you want to shop.
  • You’ll earn bonus points or cash back based on your purchase amount (usually excluding taxes and shipping fees).

Benefits for Website Owners

  • Increased Traffic: When a shopping portal partners with an online retailer, it drives traffic to the retailer’s website. Shoppers who access the retailer through the portal contribute to increased site visits.
